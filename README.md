# ☕ Pourover — A Precision Brewing Timer

A beautiful, no-nonsense pourover timer for coffee nerds. Built as a single HTML file. No app store. No subscriptions. No drama.

---

## ✨ Features

- ⏱️ **Step-by-step timer** — guides you through each pour with a countdown, so you never have to guess
- ⚖️ **Auto-scaling** — input your coffee grams and the water amounts scale automatically to your ratio
- 📖 **Built-in recipes** — includes Tetsu Kasuya's classic 4:6 method and a few variations to start
- 🧪 **Custom recipes** — build and save your own pour sequences with full control over grams, timing, and draw-down
- 🫘 **Session logging** — tag your beans, jot tasting notes, and save every brew to history
- 📱 **Works on your phone** — add it to your home screen and it runs like a native app (no App Store required)
- 💾 **Saves everything locally** — recipes and history live in your browser, no account needed

## 🫗 The 4:6 Method

Developed by World Brewers Cup champion **Tetsu Kasuya**, the 4:6 method divides your total water into two phases:

- **First 40%** — split into two pours, controls sweetness and acidity
- **Last 60%** — split into remaining pours, controls strength and body

It's a systematic approach that makes dialing in your cup actually repeatable. No more "I think it was good but I have no idea what I did."

## 🛠️ Tech

One `.html` file. Vanilla JS. Zero dependencies. Zero build steps.
